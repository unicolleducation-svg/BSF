Who we are — Introduction

The Bangladeshi Students Forum, France (BSF) is a registered association in France, operating under the French association law of 1901 (Loi du 1er juillet 1901 relative au contrat d'association). In solidarity with the Anti-discrimination Students Movement in Bangladesh, the Forum organized protests at various locations in Paris, including Place de la République, Place de la Bastille, Place de la Nation, and in front of the Eiffel Tower. We sent memorandums to international organizations and communicated developments regarding the student protests in Bangladesh to international media during the internet blackout to generate global pressure and draw attention to the situation. Following these efforts, we formally registered as an official association in France.

The Forum (BSF) is committed to welcoming and supporting students and individuals of Bangladeshi origin by providing vital information on university admissions, student accommodations, and the French visa process. We also assist Bangladeshi immigrants in France seeking to further their education at French universities. Our service-related departments are currently under development, and updates will be shared on our Facebook page as they become available.

For any inquiries, please contact us at our official email: bdstudentsforumfr@gmail.com. When reaching out, kindly introduce yourself and specify the purpose of your message. Thank you for your cooperation.
