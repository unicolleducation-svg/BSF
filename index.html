Who we are — Introduction

Bangladeshi Students Forum, France (BSF France) is a non‑profit, non‑political community connecting Bangladeshi students across French cities and universities. We run welcome programs for new arrivals, mentorship and academic support, cultural celebrations, scholarship and visa guidance, and career networking with alumni in French industry and research.
